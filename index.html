What is FarmDrive all about?

Via our simple to use web based platform,Farmers are able to register with and painlessly maintain their revenue productivity and expense records.

Farmers wishing to expand their ventures are able to apply for funding via our platform.Their records combined with other bio-data are provide them a credit score.
